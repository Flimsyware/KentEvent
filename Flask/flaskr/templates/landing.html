Meet the Team!

We are Flimsyware! I company comprised of 14 Computer Science students at Kent State University. Our vision is to to unite the campus and help students find their passion. We created Kent Event to bring people closer together and open them up to new safe events on the Kent State Campus.

Look for Fun Events!

By registering and making an account, users will be allow to view events happening on campus! Events are posted by users that are considered to be "Hosts". These students are heads of University sectioned clubs and events to ensure that the events posted are safe.If you believe that you are a Host, make sure that you check the Host box during registeration.

Make Connections!

Users can search for certain events, and filter for catergories;however, the goal of KentEvent is explore new possibilites! Try something new and meet people that you might not normally interact with. Sign up today!
